Mote Kyar Si

Description

Among desserts with sweetened syrup, Mote Sate Chi is the most straightforward to make. Chwey tiny balls with sweet cool soup could make you feel fresh in the heat of summer.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Jaggery
  • Coconut flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil jaggery in water to get the thin syrup.
  2. Mix the glutinous rice flour with water and make tiny balls out of it.
  3. Cook them in the boiling water and if they float, it's ready to mix them with the jaggery syrup we prepared earlier and eat.
