Mote Let Kout

Photo credit to the original uploader

Description

Mote Let Kout or Burmese Donut is one of the trending desserts among local people. Slightly crispy outer layer with soft chewy texture inside, when you dip it in the syrup, it makes it more flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Jaggery

Steps

  1. Boil jaggery in water to get the thin syrup.
  2. Mix the glutinous rice flour with water and make it in the shape of a donut before frying it.
