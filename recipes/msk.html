Mote Si Kyaw

Description

Mote Si Kyaw, also known as Sticky rice pancake is a sweet and crispy(optional) burmese dessert. Due to its simple and fast recipe, it's another popular choice among burmese desserts.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour
  • Jaggery
  • Peanut (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil jaggery in water to get the thick syrup.
  2. Mix the syrup with glutinous rice flour. Add some water if needed, but the paste shouldn't be too runny.
  3. While frying the pancake, shower it with tiny pieces of nuts. (Peanut, almond nuts, etc...). You can also use sesame or poppy seed, according to your preference.
